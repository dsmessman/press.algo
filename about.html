About press.algo

press.algo is a pay-per-view content service that offers a simple and efficient way for consumers to access high-quality content while also providing creators with a fair commission for their work.

Our service operates on a pay-per-view model, where consumers are charged $0.02 for each view of a piece of content. This allows for a more sustainable revenue model for creators, as they receive 50% commission for each view their content receives.

At press.algo, we are committed to creating a platform that benefits both consumers and creators. We believe in the power of great content to entertain, educate, and inspire, and we are dedicated to making it accessible and rewarding for everyone involved.
