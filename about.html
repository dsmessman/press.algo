About press.algo

press.algo is a pay-per-view content service that allows creators to share their content with the world. Our platform uses Algorand for a simple and secure payment system for both creators and consumers, with consumers being charged $0.02 per view and creators receiving $0.01 commission.

Our goal is to provide a platform that benefits both consumers and creators, giving consumers access to unique and high-quality content while directly supporting their favorite creators.

If you have any questions about press.algo or our services, please visit our contact us page.
